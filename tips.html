General Rules

Competition Day

Try to get to the competition on the first day and as early as possible. it is necessary to check the registration of all members to access the competitor area. Make sure that all team members are correctly registered in the "Sistema Olimpo" and with their recent photos, which highlight the face. Early arriving means more time to organize your tools and more warm-up time.

Robots

The competition can be hectic and stressful. It is strongly recommended to have more than 3 robots and, if not complete, at least spare parts of the robots. Due to the time, many games take place with little time, so the batteries are particularly important to have the maximum capacity for competition in all games. Practice and setup easy solutions to common problems, game position strategies and to be a referee.

Venue

The venue is always noisy. Be prepared for small variations in the lighting and quality of the competition fields. The camera may be slightly offset from the middle of the field, be prepared to always have to recalibrate your vision system.

Network

The organization does not guarantee network points or a Wi-Fi signal. Bring everything ready and always have backups available locally. Try to know in advance the location of the competition, as it may be far from the team's hotels or from any shopping center, which makes it difficult to access the internet during the games.
